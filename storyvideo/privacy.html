Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the StoryVideo mobile application (the "App"), published by Crazylabs ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use it. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in, we collect basic account details such as your email address and a user identifier through our authentication provider.
  • Content you provide. The stories you paste, the scene prompts you edit, and the videos you generate. This content is processed to produce your videos.
  • Usage & diagnostic data. Anonymous analytics and crash data that help us understand app performance and fix problems.
  • Purchase information. If you buy credits, the transaction is handled by Google Play. We receive confirmation of the purchase but not your full payment card details.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate videos from the stories and prompts you provide.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • To manage your account, credits, and purchases.
  • To diagnose issues, prevent abuse, and keep the service secure.

3. Third-party services

The App relies on trusted third-party services to function, which may process data on our behalf, including Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, analytics, and app integrity), Google Play Billing (for purchases), and a video-generation provider used to create your clips. These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

4. Content processing and storage

Story text and prompts you submit are sent to our video-generation services to create clips. Generated videos and related project data may be stored so you can access them across sessions. Video stitching is performed on your device.

5. Data retention

We retain your account and content data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the address below.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Your choices

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the App. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at atulvasudev2008@gmail.com.
